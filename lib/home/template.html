alrt

A timer.
No nonsense, fast & clean.
Just how it should be.

Just enter the timer duration you want & hit enter.
Examples: 1h (you will get one hour), 10m (is 10 minutes)
or: 50s (is 50 seconds).

Or simply type your duration into the URL, like this: http://alrt.io/1h3s
Examples: 1 hour & 3 seconds or 2 minutes & 50 seconds
